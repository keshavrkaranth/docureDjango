MISSIONS

  1. Create awareness among the public about the importance of regional food habits for healthy leaving.
  2. Provide easy access to natural,organic unprocessed food products through online booking and doorstep delivery.
  3. Promote healthy cold pressed edible oils in place of chemically refined and adulterated vegetable oils.
  4. Promote healthy complex crab based millets in place of simple crab based grains.
