WHY NATURAL, UNPROCESSED FOOD?

  • To improve overall well-being, let’s first understand the concept of “wholefoods” versus “fragmented” or processed foods.

  • Processed foods have been stripped of one or more of their original elements, i.e. they are missing valuable nutrients, fibres or water.

  • Natural foods, or whole foods (whole grains, seeds, nuts, beans and legumes) have been provided for us by nature. They are the perfect foods for our body for healthy living.

  • Whilst we can certainly exist on processed foods, we cannot survive on these foods alone.

  • Man-made/factory-made processed foods, which have been highly sugared, salted and processed, are devoid of so many of the nutrients and life-giving nutrients for optimal health.

  • Consumption of highly processed foods and white flour-based products having a high glycemic index and glycemic load, leads to accumulation of body fat and weight gain, and causes many chronic diseases.

  • Doesn’t it make sense to move toward nature and simply eat natural foods
